ABOUT US

From what started as a small group of passionate fashion loving individuals in North Brunswick, New Jersey in 2008, to a multinational team, SHOPIFY has grown immensely to what it is today. The SHOPIFY girl is fashion forward yet classy, effortlessly cool and unafraid to venture into different styles. SHOPIFY prides itself on offering on-trend styles catering to both young women and teens, that won’t break the bank. SHOPIFY is able to stay on top of the latest fashion trends from around the globe while rapidly bringing these styles to market. So whether you’re searching for boho dresses and graphic tees or patterned blouses and chic swimwear, SHOPIFY is the ultimate one-stop-shop for the modern yet economical fashionista.
